About

Hi. My name is Shayan, and currently, I am a Master of Digital Media student at the University of British Columbia. Before coming to Canada, I finished my Bachelor’s in Computer Engineering at the Iran University of Science and Technology. Also, I have more than three years of experience working as a software engineer at Informatics Services Corporation, the primary banking service provider in the country.

In addition, I was a research assistant for more than three years at the Bio-Inspired System Design Lab under the supervision of Prof. Soroush Sadeghnejad. Before that, I worked under the supervision of Prof. Mohsen Bahrami at the Robotics laboratory at the Amirkabir University of Technology.

I am mainly interested in working on Cloud Computing, Distributed Systems, and Blockchain.
